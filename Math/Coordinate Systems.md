# Polar Coordinates

## Polar to Cartesian

$$x=r\cos(\theta)$$
$$y=r\sin(\theta)$$

## Cartesian to Polar

$$r^2=x^2+y^2$$
$$tan(\theta)=\frac{y}{x}$$

# Cylindrical Coordinates
## Cylindrical to Cartesian

$$x=r\cos(\theta)$$
$$y=r\sin(\theta)$$
$$z=z$$

## Cartesian to Cylindrical

$$r^2=x^2+y^2$$
$$\tan(\theta)=\frac{y}{x}$$
$$z=z$$
